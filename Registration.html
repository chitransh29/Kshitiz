ADMISSIONS ARE OPEN FOR ALL THE CLASSES FROM NURSERY TO CLASS IX AND XI

REGISTRATION

(A).Pre-Primary.
(i).The Age Limit as on Ist April is for Nursery 3+ , K.G.-I. 4+, and K.G.-II., 5+.
(ii).Qualifying in an oral test as well as in interview is required for gaining admission to Nursery .
 (B).Primary:
The minimum age limit for Class-I (the first stage of primary) is 6+.It goes on in the same way for other classes. There wiil be admission test and interview for candidates seeking admission in primary classes.
Admission test will be taken in English, Hindi and Mathematics. A child who qualifies in the written test and interview but fails to fulfill the required age limit will not be given admission to the class which he/she desires.
 (C).Junior:
Admission to junior classes will be permitted only after qualifying in a written test and and in subsequent interview.
Written test will be taken in curricular subjects like English,Mathematics,Science, and G.K.
 (D). Secondary:
A child must qualify in written test and in subsequent interview for admission in Class-IX.
The subjects for written test are English,Mathematics,Science and G.K.
(i)No admission is made to class X.
(E).SENIOR SECONDARY:
(i). Admission to class XI is grted in may / june just after the declaration of boards result .A student who secure 75% marks in English,Mathematics,Science in CBSE of class X and 80% marks in these subjectsin the case ICSE , no matter what is the total pecentage he /she secured in board exam,is eligible to register his/her name for the entrance test.
Entrance test will be taken in English,Mathematics,Physics,Chemistry,Biology and Mental ability. The candidates should qualify both in the written test and interview for considering their admission in Class XI.
(ii). Out of the subject opted at the time of admission by the students of class-XI can change subject once latest by 31st July. In the case the details for registration , e.t.c. are send to CBSE earlier than 31st July , no request for change in subject can be entertained.
(iii).Candidates taking admission in pre-primary/primary shall be valid upto class-V. For promotion to class-VI either the students shall have to secure minimum 60% of marks in all subjects in class-V annual examination have to sit in the entance test meant for outside students for admission in class-VI.
