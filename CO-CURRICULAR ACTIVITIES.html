CO-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

The school offers a variety of hobbies and activities. Every student is supposed to take part and co-operate in varios school activities such as debating,delivering speech,essay writing , quiz etc. Additional arrangements for music, art, dance, model making, clay work, pottery, gardening, stitching and embridery etc.have been for students.These activities ultimately shape the balanced personality in an individual when from adolescence to adulthood.
