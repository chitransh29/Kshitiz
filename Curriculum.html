CURRICULUM

The course of study followed in the school is in accordance with the syllabus prescribed by the C.B.S.E.,Delhi. The curriculum includes the following subjects:

PRE-PRIMARY :

English,Hindi,Maths,G.K.,Drawing.

 Class I & II :

English,Hindi,Maths,S.S.T.,Computer, General Knowledge,Drawing,Craft.

 Class III to V :

English,Hindi,Maths,Elementary Science,Social Studies,Computer,G.K.,Drawing,Craft

 Class VI to VIII :

English.Hindi,Maths,Science,S.S.T.,Computer,G.K.,Sanskrit,Drawing,Craft,S.U.P.W and Physical Education

 Class IX & X :

English Communicative,Hindi Course-A,Maths,Science & Technology,Social Science,S.U.P.W., and Physical Education.

 Class XI & XII :

a) Core language :
(1)English
(2)Hindi

 b) Elective Subject :

(1)Maths
(2)Hindi
(3)Chemistry
(4) Biology
(5) Computer Science
