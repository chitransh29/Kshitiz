ADMISSIONS ARE OPEN FOR ALL THE CLASSES FROM NURSERY TO CLASS IX AND XI

TRANSFER CASES

1. Officers who are posted at Gorakhpur on transfer may seek admission of their wards in our school.
2. Their cases are considered subject to availability of seats and fulfulling of the conditions that thay submit their applications alongwith photocopies of their trasfer orders and report card of the last Institution in respect of thier wards and that too within 15 days of the issue of transfer orders or 7 days of assumption of charge at Gorakhpur .
3. Admission process shall be as in the case of general candidates as laid down in the prospectus .
4. Entrance test will be held once in the last week of April in respect of the candidates whose applications are recieved upto one day earlier of the date of test. Similarly test may be conducted in the last week of July in the same process of the first test.
(i) No test will be conducted after July.
(ii) No test will be held for individual cases.
(iii)The date of test will be notified on the notice board. Changes may take place if necessary.
(iv) In the absence of any vacant seat, no admission can take place.
(v) For further information, office may be contacted.
