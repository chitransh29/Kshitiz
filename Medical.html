ADMISSIONS ARE OPEN FOR ALL THE CLASSES FROM NURSERY TO CLASS IX AND XI

Medical Facalities

1.The school has opened a homeopathic clinic to provide medical facilities to the students.
2.An expert doctor's services will be available between 7:30 a.m. to 9:30 a.m. on all working days and on fridays, apart from morning the doctor will be available in the afternoon also.
3.All the facilities have been arranged to give the students high level knowedge about the First Aid with the help of all necessary equipments besides giving knowledge about health and hygiene, balanced diet, cleanliness, physical education e.t.c.

(i). Parents are advised to intimate class teachers in writing if their wards have been suffering from diseases like jaundice,typhoid,e.t.c. and are joining the school after recovery so that due care can be taken for them.

(ii).For Pre-Primary and primary students, if guardians require that their wards are to be given medicines during school hours, they should inform the respective class teachers so that follow up action can be arranged through the First Aid Incharge.

(iii). In the case students feel uneasiness or develop some sort of dieseases during school time,they should report to the First Aid Room where the incharge will take care of them and if necessary , will the call the guardian over the phone to take them home. In the event of serious illness, the school will itself take the ailing student to the nearest hospital and after treatment will take him/her home through the school vehicle.

For the above purpose it is necessary to provide the school, the local guardian's name, contact number e.t.c.
and if the student is staying in hostel, warden's name, contact number e.t.c.
