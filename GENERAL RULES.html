ADMISSIONS ARE OPEN FOR ALL THE CLASSES FROM NURSERY TO CLASS IX AND XI

RULES AND REGULATIONS

1.Students must come come to school before the first assembly bell at 8:55 a.m.The school gate will be closed at 9:00 a.m.Late comers won't be allowed to enter the school.
2.Students are not allowed to come to school by riding scooter or moped.
3.Keep a sense of self-respect in your dress and behaviour.
4.Students should not bring money from their home unnecessarily.
5.Respect your elders and teachers, stand up whenever you see them.You should be polite and well behaved in the school compound and outside
6.You should talk polite and obliging in all your dealings with others.
7.Keep a sense of self respect.
8.Make use of the wprds 'please' and 'thanks' more often.Avoid using indecent language.
9.Students must not make a noise in the class as well as in the school compound to maintain discipline.
10.Students must not walk about in the verandah and around the class room during the class hours.
11.Students must not throw paper or food stuff anywhere in the school premises except in the trashcan provided for the purpose.
12.Students must not write or scratch on the school walls or deface the school furniture.
13.Absentess wouldn't be allowed to enter the class without the permission of the principal.Leave application with parent's signature must be submitted in the principal office.
14.Sudents shall carry their lunch boxes along with them.Lunch box shall noy be accepted by the office for passing on the same to the particular student any under any circumstances.
16.The hair of girls should be cut small,long hair should be tied in double plates with black ribbon.Coloured pins,coloured hair bands,coloured ribbons and colourful ear rings are not allowed.
17.Girls are not allowed to use gold and silver ornaments.School will not be responsible if any costly item is lost.
18.Students up to class VI are not allowed to use wrist watch.
19.Absolute silence must be observed during the assembly and while moving in the class.
20.Pupils are not allowed to enter the staff room.
21.Articles found in the school premises are to handled over to the office.
22.White uniform is must on Saturday.
23.To maintain fluency in English,English should be spoken by the students in and around the school.
24.During class hours students are not allowed to go home or elsewhere.In case of emergent cases parents of students has to give application for short leave .However the decision wiil at the descirtion of the Principal as per the importance of the subject.
